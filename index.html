At Enveda, we're constructing and decoding the largest integrated dataset of anthropological, biological, and chemical information from medicinal plants that humanity has used for thousands of years to inspire new medicines. Our platform is systematizing the serendipity behind the discovery of drugs like Aspirin, Quinine, Metformin, and CBD. By systematically probing the "dark" bio-chemical space of medicinal plants, which humanity has narrowed over millennia of trial and error, we're uncovering safe molecules with remarkable polypharmacology that will herald a new generation of drugs from the roots of medicine itself. The possibilities are endless - from new antibiotics to hypotensives.

We're building a team of bold thinkers that isn't afraid to reimagine drug discovery at the interface of knowledge engineering, unbiased biology, and cutting-edge metabolomics. If this sounds like you, please drop us a note.
